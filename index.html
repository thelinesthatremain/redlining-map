About this map

Maps the legacy of 1930s mortgage "redlining" in Alabama. The Home Owners' Loan Corporation (HOLC) graded neighborhoods A–D for lending risk; only Birmingham, Montgomery & Mobile were surveyed in Alabama. Colored zones show those grades; counties show present-day outcomes.

Sources: HOLC grades & boundaries — Mapping Inequality, Univ. of Richmond (CC BY-NC). Demographics, home values, poverty — U.S. Census Bureau, ACS 2023 5-year. Loan denials — CFPB/FFIEC HMDA, 2023. County boundaries — Census TIGERweb.

Method & caveats: HOLC-zone figures average the 2023 ACS census tracts whose center falls within each grade (spatial join). County figures are county-wide averages, so a county like Jefferson blends its redlined core with affluent suburbs — the sharpest gaps appear at the neighborhood (zone) level. Denial rate = denied ÷ (denied + originated), all loan purposes.
